The Garden of Forking Paths

3

Recognizing the name of one of our consuls, I replied, somewhat taken aback.

"Your ancestor? Your illustrious ancestor? Come in."

The damp path zigzagged like those of my childhood. When we reached the house, we went into a library filled with books from both East and West.

"The garden?" "The garden of forking paths." Something stirred in my memory and I said, with incomprehensible assurance: "The garden of my ancestor, Ts'ui Pen."
